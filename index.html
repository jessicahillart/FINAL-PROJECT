GET TO KNOW ME

Web Design, Graphic Design, Branding and Identity, SEO Sensible.

I am a design nerd who loves typography and infographics. I am lucky enough to make a living doing this. I ended up learning HTML and CSS because I wanted to make websites that not only worked well, but looked good, too. I love a good challenge. I grew up in New York, went to college at the University of the Arts in Philadelphia, and ended up in Nashville with my husband and two dogs. So, yeah, I am pretty much kicking ass.

I have done work for clients suck as Nick jr, Hot Topic, Shirt Woot and more. I currently work full time at the Nashville Entrepreneur Center doing web and Graphic Design, and part time for General Assembly as a Mentor for their Web Design course.
